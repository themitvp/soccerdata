The focus will now be on the soccer players’ Wikipedia pages. What kind of data can be extracted and how much does it actually say about the soccer players?

Can the players’ position on the field or their market value be predicted alone by their Wikipedia page?

To answer these questions, two classifiers are created and trained with a small fragment of the soccer players' Wikipedia corpus.

Lastly, there is created a word cloud from the Wikipedia page for each of the soccer players Neymar, Messi and Ronaldo. Furthermore, these word clouds are investigated to find out how informative they are about the soccer players.

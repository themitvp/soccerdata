This section will investigate whether a classifier can trained to predict soccer players’ position on field by only using the their Wikipedia page.

The massive Wikipedia corpus of the soccer players’ pages have been tokenized, stemmed and narrowed down to a bag-of-words, which was used to train the classifier. Additionally, a training set of the corpus labelled with the correct positions was used to further train the classifier.

The classifier’s predictions of the field positions of 10 soccer players are presented in the table below.

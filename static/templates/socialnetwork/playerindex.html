Lord Bendtner Index

To find out if the six degrees of separation existed in the soccer player network, the Danish soccer player Nicklas Bendtner was used as the index.

The distances from Bendtner to all other players were measured and plotted in the histogram below. The average distance was then calculated and displayed below.

Average Bendtner Index:

2,7

The Bendtner Index is at its highest 5, which complies with the maximum of six degrees of separation. Most players are 3-4 steps away from him, but an average of 2,7 indicates that Bendtner has a central role in the network.

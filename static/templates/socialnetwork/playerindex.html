Lord Bendtner Index

To examine whether the six degrees of separation apply within the soccer player network, the Danish soccer player Nicklas Bendtner was used as an index.

The distances between Bendtner and all other players were measured and plotted in the histogram below. The average distance was then calculated and displayed next to the histogram.

Average Bendtner Index:

2,7

The Bendtner Index can reach a maximum of 5, which complies with the maximum value of the six degrees of separation. Most players are 3-4 steps away from him, but an average of 2,7 indicates that Bendtner plays a central role within the network.

Given that the world is becoming increasingly globalized, higher levels of ethnic diversity become observable within different countries. Hence, an examination of the various types of communities that exist within a soccer player network proves to be particularly intriguing.

This section will investigate whether the same tendency for diversity occurs within the soccer player network. The findings are expected to point to communities of high ethnic diversity.

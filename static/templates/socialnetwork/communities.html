Communities

It will be interesting to see what types of communities there exists in a soccer player network. As the world is getting more globalized, there is a higher diversity of nationalities among the countries.

This section will investigated if the same tendency occurs in the soccer player network. This should result in communities with a diverse group of nationalities.

The modularity was calculated to examine how good the network is divided into communities.

With a modularity of 0.7, there is a strong indicator that there exist communities in the network.

22 communities were found and are displayed in the graph below:

A color represents a community. The communities overlap to a certain extent, reflected by not being completely separated.

For further investigation, the nodes are kept in their position but the colors now represents countries. The graph below then reflects if the countries correspond to the communities.

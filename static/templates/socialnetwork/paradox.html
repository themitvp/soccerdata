Friendship Paradox

This section will explore two questions: firstly, whether the well-known network friendship paradox also occurs in the soccer player network or not; and secondly, whether the average market value of a soccer player's linked connections is higher than his own market value.

For the purpose of the first question, it was examined whether a soccer player is connected with other soccer players who, on average, have a higher number of connections.

For the purpose of the second question, the market value was now added as a parameter of the friendship paradox, in order to show whether soccer players generally play with other players of a higher market value.

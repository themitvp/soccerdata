What Does the Degree Distribution Show?

The histogram indicates the existence of certain hubs within the network. Soccer players with a high degree distribution are less frequent than those with a lower one. Additionally, the histogram demonstrates that most soccer players have an approximately 50-degree distribution. This means that most soccer players play with roughly 50 other players in the course of their career.

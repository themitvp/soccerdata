A network of soccer players has been created and analysed. The Wikipedia pages of all the soccer players have been investigated and used to create classifiers.

Which other interesting discoveries can be found in the massive soccer player data set? Can it reveal which clubs, relative to the amount they spent, did the best transfers, e.g. by calculating the relative profit? Or what is the all time best player transfers?

Next, the hypothesis that the Italian soccer club win with fewer goals than everybody else is investigated.
